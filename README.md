# Revenue & Operational Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard solution designed to support executive decision-making and operational performance monitoring for a video-generation SaaS business.

The dashboard focuses on analysing revenue growth, customer engagement, marketing channel performance, subscription tier performance, GPU usage, customer satisfaction, and infrastructure efficiency.

## Business Objectives

* Analyse revenue performance across industries, regions, marketing channels, and subscription tiers.
* Identify high-performing customer segments and revenue growth opportunities.
* Monitor operational efficiency, GPU consumption, provider performance, and success rates.
* Support data-driven decision-making through clear and interactive Power BI reporting.

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* Microsoft Excel
* Data Visualisation
* KPI Reporting
* Business Analytics

## Dashboard 1: Executive Growth & Revenue Performance

This dashboard provides insights into:

* Total revenue
* Average revenue per customer
* Monthly revenue growth trends
* Revenue contribution by marketing channel
* Revenue by industry and region
* Customer engagement by subscription tier
* Template pricing and revenue generation

## Dashboard 2: Operational Efficiency & Infrastructure Performance

This dashboard provides insights into:

* Average GPU consumption
* Videos per GPU second
* Success rate
* Average customer rating
* GPU demand by template complexity and pricing
* GPU resource usage by template category
* GPU provider performance
* Top-performing templates by success rate

## Key Insights

* LinkedIn and referral channels contributed strongly to revenue performance.
* E-commerce generated the highest revenue across industries.
* Enterprise customers showed stronger engagement and revenue potential.
* Complex templates required higher GPU consumption, creating infrastructure cost considerations.
* Provider performance varied by success rate and render time, supporting future optimisation opportunities.

## Recommendations

* Prioritise high-performing channels such as LinkedIn and referrals.
* Focus customer growth strategies on high-value industries and subscription tiers.
* Monitor GPU-heavy templates to manage infrastructure cost and scalability.
* Use provider performance metrics to support infrastructure planning and vendor optimisation.

## Project Value

This project demonstrates the ability to build Power BI dashboards that combine customer, revenue, marketing, and operational data into actionable business insights.
